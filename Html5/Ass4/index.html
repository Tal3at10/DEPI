About Chainsaw Man

Chainsaw Man is a wild, visceral anime that blends intense action with dark humor and emotional depth. Following the story of Denji, a young man who merges with his devil-dog Pochita to become the Chainsaw Man, the series delivers a unique take on the shonen genre with its unapologetic violence and surprisingly heartfelt moments.

In a world where Devils are born from human fears, Devil Hunters risk their lives to exterminate them. Denji, a poverty-stricken young man, makes a contract with his pet devil Pochita and gains the ability to transform parts of his body into chainsaws. Recruited by Makima of the Public Safety Devil Hunters, Denji finds himself fighting terrifying devils while navigating his newfound powers and desires.

Main Characters

Denji

The protagonist who becomes Chainsaw Man after merging with Pochita. His simple desires and brutal combat style make him unforgettable.

Power

The Blood Fiend and Denji's chaotic teammate. Selfish yet endearing, she forms an unlikely bond with Denji.

Aki Hayakawa

A serious Devil Hunter who initially dislikes Denji but eventually becomes something like an older brother figure.

Makima

The mysterious and powerful leader of Public Safety's Devil Hunters. Her true motives remain unclear throughout much of the story.
